Rings of Time

How to play

You start out with 15 seconds to move around. You want to get the clock by jumping(moving to a close circle), and moving around the circle. To jump, you click on a close circle, but you must be on the half facing the circle you are jumping to. There is a delay when you get your clock, so you must have enough time to account for the split-second delay. Each clock gives you 5 more seconds. The clocks have a longer respawn time when you obtain the clock. Your goal is to survive for the longest time possible

  • a: move clockwise around the circle
  • d: move counterclockwise around the circle
  • Click: jump(can't jump diagonally)
